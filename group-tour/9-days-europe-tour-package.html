Itinerary Details

Day 1

Arrival in Oslo

Arrive in Oslo, Norway. Transfer to your hotel for check-in and settle in.

In the evening, head out for a casual stroll around Karl Johans Gate and the Oslo Waterfront to get a feel of the city's atmosphere.

Enjoy a delicious halal dinner at a local halal restaurant offering Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisine.

Meals:

Dinner

Day 2

Explore Oslo

Have breakfast at your hotel. Get ready for a day of exploration.

Visit the iconic Vigeland Sculpture Park, home to over 200 sculptures by Gustav Vigeland.

Continue to the Oslo Opera House and enjoy panoramic views of the city and fjord.

Explore Akershus Fortress and the surrounding waterfront area.

In the evening, enjoy dinner at a halal restaurant serving authentic Middle Eastern cuisine.

Activities:

Vigeland Sculpture Park

Oslo Opera House

Akershus Fortress

Breakfast

Dinner

Day 3

Travel to Brussels

After breakfast, check out from your hotel.

Travel from Oslo to Brussels.

Upon arrival, check in to your Brussels hotel.

In the evening, enjoy dinner at "La Maison de la Méditerranée", offering a mix of Mediterranean and North African halal dishes.

Meals:

Breakfast

Dinner

Day 4

Explore Brussels

Have breakfast at your hotel.

Visit the iconic Atomium, a unique architectural wonder. The museum inside offers exhibitions related to design and culture.

In the afternoon, explore Brussels at your own pace with the Hop-On Hop-Off tour. The bus stops at various key sites like the Grand Place, the Royal Palace, and the European Parliament.

In the evening, head to "Restaurant Al-Jazeera" for an authentic halal meal, offering a range of Middle Eastern and Mediterranean dishes.

Activities:

Atomium Entry Ticket

Hop-On Hop-Off Tour

Travel to Paris by bus/train.

Check into your Paris hotel.

Breakfast

Explore Paris

Have breakfast at your hotel. Get ready for a full day of sightseeing.

Visit the magnificent Notre-Dame Cathedral and learn about its history and architectural beauty.

Head to the Eiffel Tower and take an elevator ride to the second floor or summit for spectacular views of Paris.

In the evening, dinner at "Le Restaurant Al-Mounia", serving Moroccan and Algerian halal dishes.

Explore Madrid

Have breakfast at your hotel. Prepare for a day full of iconic Madrid attractions.

Visit the legendary Real Madrid Stadium, with access to exclusive areas like the trophy room, players' tunnel, and the pitch.

Explore the opulent Madrid Royal Palace, one of the largest and most beautiful palaces in Europe. Learn about the Spanish monarchy and admire the architecture.

Dinner at "Restaurante Arabe Alhambra" for a special halal meal with Moroccan and Andalusian flavors.

Visa Requirements
Visa Requirements for
Business Owner

Identification Documents
  • CNIC (National ID Card) scanned colored copy
  • Passport-Original and all previous passports
  • Three recent colored 35 x 45 mm photographs on a white background
  • FRC (Family Registration Certificate) from NADRA

Financial Document
  • Solo travelers need a 6-month personal bank statement (minimum balance PKR 1,000,000) on the bank's letterhead, including bank details, and for families (minimum PKR 2,500,000). Avoid sudden deposits
  • Personal Income Tax Returns of last 3 years

Supporting Documents
  • Proof of Proprietorship / Partnership (In case the applicant is a Proprietor / Partner in a firm)
  • Company Registration Certificate / Certificate of Incorporation
  • Chamber of Commerce Certificate if you have a membership with the Chamber of Commerce
  • Company Income Tax returns for the last 3 years (If filed separately)
  • Original Business Current Bank Account Statement for last 6 months mentioning the Business Name and Account No. on the Bank’s letterhead with the seal and sign of the bank officer.
  • Relative/s in Belgium (if any)
